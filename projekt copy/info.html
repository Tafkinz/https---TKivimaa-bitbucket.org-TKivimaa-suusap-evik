Clan info

Informaton of the participating teams

T3

Started in 2005 by Tafka, Crusa and Raffa it is one of the oldest and top playered clans around the league, It is known for it's aggressive style and great skills in classic, set in their home base T3 resort.

OfO

OfO is one of the newest teams around, set together in the late 2014. It is known for defensive playstyle, mastering different tactics and using all kinds of glitches to their advantage. Mainly focuses on tight areas.

Bbk

Bbk started around 2010 by Morta, Rifla and Gaussa. It is true to their idea to play for fun and give everything to have fun around the league, teaching new teams how to make interesting tricks and play efficiently the same time.
